The Library by the River

A traveler found a quiet library beside the river after walking through the rain for most of the afternoon.

The librarian gave him a dry towel, a cup of tea, and a map of the town that had been drawn by hand many decades earlier.

When the traveler opened the map, he noticed a small mark near the bridge that was not shown on any modern guide.
